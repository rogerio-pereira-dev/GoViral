# 1. Brand Core

# 2. Brand Positioning

GoViral is a tech-driven AI tool built for creators who want clarity,
speed, and strategic advantage.

It is not a marketing agency.\
It is not a coaching program.\
It is not a course.

It is a growth engine designed to generate actionable insights in
minutes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Brand Archetype

Primary Archetype: - The Hacker

Secondary Archetypes: - The Strategist - The Modern Creator

Brand Identity Essence: A sharp, energetic, intelligent AI-powered
growth tool.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Emotional Positioning

Primary Emotions:

-   Hope
-   Speed
-   Confidence

When users interact with GoViral, they should feel:

-   Growth is possible
-   Momentum is immediate
-   The system is reliable and intelligent

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Tone of Voice

## Core Tone

-   Modern
-   Tech-forward
-   Friendly
-   Neutral
-   Energetic

## Voice Characteristics

-   Direct but not aggressive
-   Confident but not arrogant
-   Inspirational but not hype-driven
-   Smart but accessible

## Example Messaging

Headline Example: Engineered for Viral Growth.

Subheadline Example: Turn insight into viral momentum in minutes.

CTA Primary: Start My Growth

CTA Secondary: Generate My Growth Blueprint

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Visual Identity

## Design Direction

-   Dark mode foundation
-   Neon highlights
-   Futuristic aesthetic
-   Energetic but clean

## Color System

Primary Pink: #FE2C55

Primary Teal: #25F4EE

Background Base: dark charcoal ( #121212 )

Accent Use: Neon glow effects on CTA and highlights

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Typography System

Headline Font: Space Grotesk

Body Font: Inter

Usage Guidelines:

-   Headlines: Bold, high contrast
-   Body: Clean and readable
-   Use uppercase for strong CTA emphasis sparingly
-   Maintain strong visual hierarchy

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Logo Guidelines

Structure: Symbol + Wordmark

Conceptual Elements:

-   Viral wave motion
-   Lightning / acceleration symbol

Design Principles:

-   Dynamic motion
-   Clean geometry
-   Neon gradient (Teal to Pink)
-   Scalable for favicon and app icon

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. UI Guidelines

-   High contrast layouts
-   Smooth hover transitions
-   Subtle glow on interactive elements
-   Animated micro-interactions for modern feel
-   Minimal clutter

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Brand Do's and Don'ts

## Do

-   Be confident
-   Be clear
-   Be fast
-   Emphasize growth and clarity
-   Keep messaging actionable

## Don't

-   Promise guaranteed virality
-   Sound like a guru
-   Overuse marketing buzzwords
-   Be corporate or overly formal
-   Overcomplicate messaging

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 11. Microcopy Guidelines

Examples:

Instead of: Submit
Use: Start My Growth

Instead of: Generate Report
Use: Generate My Growth Blueprint

Instead of: Processing
Use: Analyzing Your Growth Potential...

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 12. Brand Manifesto

Creators deserve clarity.

Growth should not be confusing.  
Strategy should not take months to learn.  
Momentum should not depend on luck.

GoViral was built to transform insight into action. To replace guesswork
with structure. To give creators a sharp, fast, smart advantage.

Engineered for Viral Growth.
